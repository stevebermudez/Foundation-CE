Florida CE hours

After first renewal

For both real estate sales associates and brokers in Florida:
	•	Total: 14 hours of continuing education every 2 years
	•	3 hours Core Law
	•	3 hours Ethics and Business Practices
	•	8 hours Specialty / Elective

First renewal after initial licensure

This is post-licensing, not standard CE:
	•	Sales associate: 45 hours post-licensing
	•	Broker: 60 hours post-licensing

Suggested catalog structure for your CE platform

Here is a clean schema you can use to distinguish courses in your system.

Core classification fields

For each course, store:
	1.	state
	•	Example: "FL"
	2.	license_type
	•	"Sales Associate"
	•	"Broker"
	•	"Sales Associate & Broker" (for shared CE)
	3.	requirement_cycle_type
	•	"Post-Licensing"
	•	"Continuing Education (Renewal)"
	4.	requirement_bucket
	•	"Core Law"
	•	"Ethics & Business Practices"
	•	"Specialty / Elective"
	•	"Post-Licensing Mandatory"
	5.	hours
	•	Numeric, example: 3, 8, 14, 45, 60
	6.	delivery_method
	•	"Self-Paced Online"
	•	"Live Webinar"
	•	"Classroom"
	7.	difficulty_level (optional but helpful)
	•	"Basic"
	•	"Intermediate"
	•	"Advanced"
	8.	sku or course_code
	9.	price
	10.	renewal_applicable (boolean)
	•	true for the 14 hour CE blocks
	•	false for pre-licensing or exam prep
